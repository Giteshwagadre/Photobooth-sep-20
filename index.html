About us

Owned and operated by a professional event planner. We understand the importance of photo quality, professionalism, fun and loving what we do! Our fully insured photo booth provides unlimited photo strips to all your guests. Each event has a personalized template to match the theme of your event. We offer a wide variety of glitz & glamour backdrops, as well as premiere props at no additional charge! Our photo booth brings a unique twist to events. We offer the traditional enclosed booth or the open-air photo booth to accommodate larger crowds. We pride ourselves on offering the highest quality entertainment and photos at a fair price.
